# Dog Care Guidelines

Dogs require consistent daily care to stay healthy and well-behaved.

## Walks and Exercise
- Most dogs need at least one to two walks per day.
- High-energy breeds may require longer or more frequent walks.
- Walks help with physical health and mental stimulation.

## Feeding
- Adult dogs are typically fed once or twice per day.
- Feeding should occur around the same time each day to build routine.
- Avoid intense exercise immediately after feeding.

## Medications
- Medications should be given at consistent times.
- Some medications must be given with food.
- Missed doses should be handled carefully and not doubled.

## Grooming
- Grooming frequency depends on coat type.
- Long-haired dogs may need brushing multiple times per week.
- Nail trimming and bathing should be scheduled regularly.

## Enrichment
- Mental stimulation is important.
- Puzzle toys, training sessions, and playtime reduce boredom and anxiety.
