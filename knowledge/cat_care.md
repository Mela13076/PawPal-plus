# Cat Care Guidelines

Cats benefit from structured routines but prefer flexibility and calm environments.

## Feeding
- Many cats prefer multiple small meals throughout the day.
- Feeding schedules should be consistent.
- Sudden diet changes can cause digestive issues.

## Litter Box Care
- Litter boxes should be cleaned daily.
- One litter box per cat plus one extra is recommended.
- Location should be quiet and easily accessible.

## Play and Enrichment
- Cats need short but frequent play sessions.
- Interactive toys that mimic hunting behaviors are ideal.
- Play helps prevent behavioral problems and obesity.

## Grooming
- Most cats groom themselves but may still need brushing.
- Long-haired cats require more frequent grooming.
- Regular grooming reduces hairballs.

## Medications
- Cats can be sensitive to medications.
- Always follow dosage instructions carefully.
- Giving meds during calm moments reduces stress.
