# General Pet Scheduling Tips

Good scheduling improves consistency and reduces stress for both pets and owners.

## Prioritization
- Health-related tasks (feeding, medications) should be highest priority.
- Exercise and enrichment should follow.
- Grooming tasks can often be scheduled flexibly.

## Time Management
- Short tasks can be grouped together.
- Avoid overloading a single time block.
- Leave buffer time between tasks when possible.

## Conflict Avoidance
- Do not schedule overlapping tasks.
- Avoid scheduling intense activity back-to-back.
- Consider pet energy levels at different times of day.

## Consistency
- Repeating tasks at similar times helps build routines.
- Daily recurring tasks should be predictable.
- Weekly or monthly tasks should still be planned in advance.
