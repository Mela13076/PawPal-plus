# Pet Medication Safety

Medication schedules are critical for pet health and should be followed closely.

## Timing
- Medications should be given at consistent times each day.
- Skipping doses can reduce effectiveness.
- Double dosing can be dangerous and should be avoided.

## With or Without Food
- Some medications require food to prevent stomach upset.
- Others must be given on an empty stomach.
- Always check instructions before scheduling.

## Monitoring
- Watch for side effects such as lethargy, vomiting, or appetite changes.
- Contact a veterinarian if unusual behavior occurs.

## Scheduling Tips
- Medication tasks should often be high priority.
- Avoid scheduling medications during busy or rushed periods.
- Linking meds to feeding times can improve consistency.
